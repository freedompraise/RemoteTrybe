Frequently Asked Questions

What is the duration of the program?

The training lasts 6 weeks, with classes held Fridays to Sundays.

What is the cost of the program?

The program fee is ₦32,000 or $23.

How do I confirm my registration?

Pay the program fee, upload your receipt, and receive your confirmation code upon verification.

Will I receive a certificate?

Yes, you will receive a certificate upon completion of the program.

Will there be recordings of the sessions?

Yes, recordings of the live sessions will be available for your reference.

Is a Laptop required for the program?

No, you can learn without a laptop, but eventually you will need one to work as a Virtual Assistant.

Does the program provide jobs?

No, we are a training program and do not provide jobs. Our goal is to equip you with the skills needed to succeed as a Virtual Assistant.
